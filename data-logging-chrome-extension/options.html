All data stays on your device. Use Export to save or share it. You can delete all data below.

How it works

  • Click Start logging in the popup for a tab to activate the content script.
  • Keystrokes are counted; special combos like Ctrl/Cmd+Z (undo), Ctrl/Cmd+Y or Shift+Ctrl/Cmd+Z (redo), and Ctrl/Cmd+Enter (compile attempt) are tagged.
  • Clicks on buttons labeled "Run/Compile/Execute/Build" are counted as compile attempts.
  • Periods with no activity beyond the idle threshold are logged as "idle".
  • Page JavaScript errors are captured via an injected window.onerror hook.
